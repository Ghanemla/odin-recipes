Description:

This is one of my favorite cookies of all time, although technically they're closer to a pie than a cookie.

Nothing prepares you for that intense, awesome sweet-tart lemon flavor and custard,

perfectly contrasted against that crisp, sweet buttery shortbread cookie.

For the shortbread crust

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsalted butter at room temprature
  • ¼ cup confectioners' sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ tespoon salt

For the lemon layer

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon confectioners' sugar
